What is BikeStash

Cyclists

BikeStash is a bike storing facility that allows you to safely park your bike in commercial spaces like cafes and shops. Find on the map the closest space to you, book, and drop off your bike.


Businesses

Using any extra space you have in your property you can bring in extra revenue and build relationships with customers who cycle. You set your availability and we handle check-in and check-out to make each transaction secure and as easy as possible.
